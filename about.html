Yolanda Murray — Founder & CEO

Built on Passion. Rooted in Love.

Yolanda Murray is the Founder and CEO of Who's Doing It Like This, a family-driven brand built on passion, resilience, and the power of bringing people together through food and hospitality.

Known for her creativity, determination, and authentic approach to serving others, Yolanda has created a business rooted in flavor, culture, and community. What began as a vision inspired by family, tradition, and hard work continues to grow into a brand recognized for homemade meals, meaningful experiences, and genuine connection.

Her leadership reflects strength, consistency, and a commitment to building legacy through service and entrepreneurship. Every plate served through Who's Doing It Like This represents the values the business was founded on — love, quality, and the belief that great food brings people together.

Today, the brand continues to grow as a reflection of Yolanda's vision: creating memorable experiences, uplifting community, and building something meaningful for generations to come.
